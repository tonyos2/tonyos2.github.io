How to Make a Paper Water Balloon

Source

Description:

Here is how to make a paper water ballon.

Ingredients:

  • Paper
  • Scissors, if paper isn't squared
  • Decorating tools (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Fold the paper into a triangle, cut excess paper.
  2. Fold into triangle again, getting 4 triangles when unfolded.
  3. Collapse two corners inside and form another triangle.
  4. Fold up the lower edges to the top point. Do this on both sides. Then fold in the middle corner half way, do this four times.
  5. Using the four small sections at the top of the paper fold each one into the middle corners that were folded previously.
  6. There will be a hole at the top of the balloon. Blow air into it until full.
  7. Filling the Balloon (with water)
  8. Paint balloon (optional)
  9. Be sure to use balloon shortly (if filled with water) to prevent leakage.
